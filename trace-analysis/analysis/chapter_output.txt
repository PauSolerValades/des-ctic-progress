Key finding: Follower count has NO effect on timeline starvation.
Empty-exit rates, session durations, and total actions are identical
across all indegree buckets. Zero-follower users starve at the same
rate as users with 10K+ followers. The bimodal empty-exit distribution
is not explained by network position — it is a timing effect: users
who happen to be online during active periods get content; those who
log in during quiet periods find empty timelines regardless of their
follower count. The root cause is timeline clearing on every offline
event, which resets everyone to zero backlog at session start.
